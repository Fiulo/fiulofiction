The Fear Within

April 8, 2023 Fiulo

'I couldn't believe my eyes as I watched the crowd of people, my own townspeople, gathering around the summoners on the snowy mountainscape. Their chants filled the air, summoning an evil spirit that promised a better life. "Join us, and your lives will be filled with riches and happiness," they called out to the townspeople, who were beginning to believe them. Desperate to save them, I pushed my way forward, but my words fell on deaf ears.

I tried to confront the summoners, my heart pounding in my chest. "You're deceiving these people," I said, my voice shaking with anger. "You're using their fears and desires to control them!" The summoners smirked, their eyes filled with arrogance. "And who are you to challenge us?" they asked, their power over the townspeople only growing stronger. Despite my best efforts, I couldn't break the spell they had cast on my people.

As the townspeople succumbed to the summoners' dark influence, the evil spirit grew stronger, consuming their minds and hearts. I was forced to watch in horror as my friends and neighbors, now completely under the spirit's control, turned against me, their faces twisted with hatred and fear.

Defeated and heartbroken, I fled the town, vowing to never let this tragedy be forgotten. I now travel from place to place, sharing my story and warning others of the dangers of false promises and dark forces. Though I couldn't save my own people, I am determined to prevent the same fate from befalling others. My failure serves as a constant reminder of the importance of vigilance and courage, and the need to always stand up against the darkness that threatens to consume us all.
